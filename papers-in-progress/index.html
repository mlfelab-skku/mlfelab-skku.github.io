Future directions

Beyond its current scope, PG-DPO admits natural extensions to partial equilibrium settings—including robust optimization, rough volatility, regime, taxation, Epstein-Zin utility, smooth ambiguity and belief-state dynamics—as well as general equilibrium frameworks such as mean-field control and multi-agent games.
