**PG-DPO (Pontryagin-Guided Direct Policy Optimization)** is our framework for solving
high-dimensional dynamic portfolio selection by coupling Pontryagin's maximum principle with
direct policy optimization, so that learned policies recover the structure of the underlying
stochastic control problem.
