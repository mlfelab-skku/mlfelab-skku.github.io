I am an Associate Professor at **Sungkyunkwan University (SKKU)**, working at the intersection of
**financial mathematics, machine learning, and stochastic control**. My research aims to make
financial mathematics *work* in high-dimensional, data-driven settings &mdash; building
**structure-preserving learning methods** that keep the economic and probabilistic structure of a
problem intact while scaling to realistic markets.

Our group develops and studies the **PG-DPO (Pontryagin-Guided Direct Policy Optimization)** family
of methods for dynamic portfolio selection, together with machine-learning approaches to asset
pricing. We are interested both in the theory (optimality, convergence, structure recovery) and in
making these methods practical for large numbers of assets.

#### Research interests

- **Structure-recovering reinforcement learning** for dynamic portfolio selection (PG-DPO and beyond).
- **Asset pricing** using machine-learning and econometric approaches.
