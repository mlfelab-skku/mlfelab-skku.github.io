**Jeonggyu Huh** &mdash; Associate Professor, Department of Mathematics, Sungkyunkwan University.

He works on financial mathematics, machine learning, and stochastic control, with a focus on
structure-preserving learning methods for high-dimensional portfolio optimization and asset pricing.
He received his Ph.D. and B.S. from Yonsei University, and previously held positions at KIAS and
Chonnam National University.
